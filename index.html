It's not just Coffee
It's Heli's Starbucks

Starbucks was founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, by three partners: Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker. Initially, Starbucks was a single store selling high-quality coffee beans and equipment. It wasn’t until Howard Schultz joined the company in 1982 that Starbucks transformed into a global coffeehouse brand. Schultz was inspired by the coffee culture in Italy and proposed the idea of creating a café where people could enjoy coffee drinks like espresso and cappuccinos.
